# **App Name**: MindBloom

## Core Features:

- Mood Tracker: Log daily mood using a sliding scale, emojis, or short descriptions to generate charts and insights.
- Guided Meditations: Provide a categorized library of short, guided audio sessions for anxiety relief, focus, sleep, and mindfulness.
- Digital Journal: Provide a private, secure space for journaling with optional prompts for reflection.
- Coping Tools: Offer quick, actionable exercises for stress, such as breathing exercises and distraction challenges.
- AI Chatbot Support: Offer a friendly chatbot that provides empathetic support, suggests coping mechanisms and directs the user to resources. The tool will consider the user's emotional state and previous history when choosing whether or not to offer specific suggestions.
- Resource Library: Provide articles, tips, and links to external mental health organizations and hotlines.
- Usage Analytics: Anonymously collect app usage metrics for use in A/B testing, and prioritizing feature development.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft lavender (#D1C4E9) to promote relaxation and tranquility.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) for a clean, calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted teal (#A7DBD8) for subtle highlights and interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif that combines a modern look and a little warmth or personality; suitable for headlines or body text
- Use minimalist, calming icons related to mental wellness.
- Maintain a clean, spacious layout to avoid overwhelming the user.
- Incorporate subtle animations for a gentle, engaging experience.
